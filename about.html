Why this exists

Neurodivergent people are often offered two bad options: force neurotypical routines until burnout, or accept chaos and call it personality.

I do not buy either. Neurodivergent and More exists to build a third option: calm structure that respects your brain.

✕ Force neurotypical routines → Burnout
✕ Accept chaos → No progress
✓ Calm structure → Work that fits your brain The third option

We focus on the few changes that create stability: energy management, strengths-based design, and execution systems that survive real life.

Credibility without overclaiming

Experience includes collaborations and work connected to organizations such as the European Commission, Spectrum think tank, and Thales. Specific project details can be shared when appropriate and when confidentiality allows.

This work is coaching and skill-building support. It is not medical care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Who it is for

  • Neurodivergent professionals who want structure without rigidity
  • Freelancers who want predictable delivery and less last-minute panic
  • Executives who want better emotional stability under pressure
  • People who want a work path that fits their strengths

Who it is not for

  • If you want quick hacks with zero practice
  • If you want someone to push you with guilt or pressure
  • If you are in crisis and need clinical support - this is not medical care

What to expect on a call

  1. Clarity - what is actually creating friction right now
  2. Fit - cohort, 1:1, or a company program
  3. Next step - you leave with a simple action, even if we do not work together
