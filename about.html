Why this exists

Neurodivergent people are often offered two bad options: force neurotypical routines until burnout, or accept chaos and call it personality.

I do not buy either. Neurodivergent and More exists to build a third option: a structure that holds — built around how your brain actually runs.

We focus on the few changes that create stability: energy management, working from your actual strengths, and execution systems that hold up in messy weeks.

What makes the approach different

Three things that separate this from the usual coaching package.

No coaching theater

No manipulation, no pressure tactics, no guilt trips. Honest feedback and tools you can use.

Your context first

Your schedule, your constraints, your energy. We work with what you have — not with an ideal version of your week.

Systems + tracking

Lightweight metrics so you can see what's moving. Keep what works. Drop what doesn't.

Credibility without overclaiming

Work connected to organizations including the European Commission, Spectrum think tank, and Thales. Specific project details available when confidentiality allows.

This work is coaching and skill-building support. It is not medical care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Who it is for

  • Neurodivergent professionals who want weeks that hold without being rigid
  • Freelancers who want to deliver on time without the Sunday night spiral
  • Executives who need to stay sharp under pressure without burning out
  • People building a work path around how they actually operate

Who it is not for

  • You want a quick fix that requires zero effort
  • You want someone to push you with guilt or pressure tactics
  • You are in crisis and need clinical support — this is not that

What to expect on a call

  1. Clarity - what is actually creating friction right now
  2. Fit - cohort, 1:1, or a company program
  3. Next step - you leave with a simple action, even if we do not work together
