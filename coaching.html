What we cover

Everything your brain
needs to stop crashing.

We build your full Neurodivergent OS together — in the order that matters most for you.

01

Energy management

Identify your real drain patterns, build recovery rules, and protect your week before it collapses. Work with your capacity, not against it.

02

Strength mapping

Find the conditions where you thrive. Build your work around your actual superpowers instead of brute-forcing tasks your brain resists.

03

Focus & execution

Minimum timebox. Decision hygiene. Anti-avoidance. The practical tools that make starting less painful and finishing more predictable.

04

Emotional skills (non-medical)

Tools for overload moments, conflict prep, and decompression routines. Practical skills for high-stakes situations — not therapy.

05

Work design & strategy

Job shaping, freelancer positioning, boundaries, delivery systems. Build a work life that doesn't require you to mask to survive it.

06

System stabilization

Keep what works. Drop what doesn't. Build a fast-reset protocol for when life disrupts everything — because it will.
