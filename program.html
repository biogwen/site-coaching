What's included

Six things you'll walk out with.

01

Your Neurodivergent OS

A simple, personal system for planning, focusing, recovering, and following through — one you can return to even after a bad week.

02

Energy management plan

Rules that reduce crashes, protect your week, and make your pace predictable. Built around your patterns, not someone else's morning routine.

03

Strength mapping

A clear picture of your strengths, triggers, and best conditions. Then a work style designed around those — not around what works for everyone else.

Execution tools that stick

Minimum timebox, decision hygiene, anti-avoidance, and low-friction routines. Practical. Tested. Designed to survive messy days.

06

The 8-week roadmap

What happens, week by week.

Example structure. Adapted based on your context and constraints.

1

Week 1

Baseline and clarity

Reduce noise, define what actually matters right now, create a simple week map you can follow without willpower.

2

Week 2

Energy management

Identify what drains you fastest. Set recovery rules. Build a pace that doesn't require you to burn out to see results.

3

Week 3

Focus systems

Minimum timebox, environment design, distraction boundaries. Start easier, finish more often.

4

Week 4

Execution without burnout

Short planning cycles, small actions that count, visible progress. The system that keeps running on bad days.

5

Week 5

Emotional skills (non-medical)

Tools for overload moments, conflict preparation, and decompression. Practical — not clinical.

6

Week 6

Strength mapping

Find your leverage points and the conditions where you consistently do your best work. Then design around those.

7

Week 7

Work design and strategy

Job shaping, freelancer positioning, boundaries, delivery systems. Build work that uses your strengths — not just your coping strategies.

8

Week 8

Stabilize and scale

Consolidate what worked. Build a realistic 90-day plan. Make the system relapse-proof for when life gets messy again.
