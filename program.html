The process

How it works

Five steps. No fluff. Designed for neurodivergent brains with real constraints.

01
📞

Book a call

We clarify what's creating friction right now, confirm fit, and pick the right path. You leave with a clear next step — even if we don't work together.

02
🗺️

Onboarding

We set your baseline, define your top priorities, and put simple tracking in place. No overwhelming setup — just enough to make progress visible.

03
🔁

Weekly rhythm

One theme. One practice. One real-life application per week. We keep the load low so you can actually follow through — even on hard weeks.

04
🤝

Support

Community prompts and templates every week. A 1:1 session every two weeks to adapt the system to your real life and unblock what's stuck.

05
🎯

Stabilize

Keep what works. Drop what doesn't. Build a realistic 90-day plan so the system survives after the program ends — without relying on willpower.

The 8-week roadmap

Example structure. We adapt based on your context and constraints.

Week 1 — Baseline and clarity

Reduce noise, define priorities, create a simple week map.

Week 2 — Energy management

Identify drains, set recovery rules, build a realistic pace.

Week 3 — Focus systems

Minimum timebox, environment design, distraction boundaries.

Week 4 — Execution without burnout

Short planning, small actions, visible progress.

Week 5 — Emotional skills (non-medical)

Tools for overload moments, conflict prep, decompression routines.

Week 6 — Superpowers mapping

Find leverage points and conditions where you thrive.

Week 7 — Work design and strategy

Job shaping, freelancer positioning, boundaries, delivery systems.

Week 8 — Stabilize and scale

Consolidate habits, create a realistic 90-day plan, relapse-proof the system.
